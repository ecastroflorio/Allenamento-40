# 🏋️ MASSA 40+ Workout Tracker

A progressive web app for tracking your 40+ muscle-building workouts with rest timers, progression tracking, and volume analytics.

## 📱 How to Install on Your Phone

### **Android Chrome**
1. **Open Chrome** on your Android phone
2. Go to: `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ecastroflorio/Allenamento-40/main/index.html`
3. Wait for the page to load (10-15 seconds)
4. Tap the **⋮ menu** (three dots, top right)
5. Tap **"Add to home screen"**
6. Choose a name and tap **"Add"**
7. ✅ App is now on your home screen!

### **iPhone Safari**
1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone
2. Go to: `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ecastroflorio/Allenamento-40/main/index.html`
3. Wait for the page to load
4. Tap the **share icon** (↑ arrow at bottom center)
5. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
6. Tap **"Add"**
7. ✅ App is now on your home screen!

### **Alternative Method (Both Phones)**
If the above method doesn't work, try:
1. Open: `https://ecastroflorio.github.io/Allenamento-40/`
2. Bookmark to home screen
3. Use it anytime

## ✨ Features

- **3 Workout Days** - Day A (Chest/Shoulders), Day B (Back/Biceps), Day C (Legs)
- **Rest Timer** - Visual countdown with audio alerts at 3, 2, 1 seconds
- **Progression Tracking** - Get advice when to increase weight
- **Volume Calculator** - Track total kg per session
- **Workout History** - Chart your progress over time
- **Offline Support** - Works without internet connection
- **Local Storage** - All data saved on your phone
- **No Account Needed** - Just open and start training

---

## 🎯 How to Use

1. **Select Day** - Choose A, B, or C
2. **Enter Weight & Reps** - Log each set
3. **Click Circle** - Mark sets as complete (starts rest timer)
4. **Adjust Rest Time** - Change rest between sets if needed
5. **Save Session** - Hit "SALVA SESSIONE" when done
6. **View History** - Check your progress in the history tab
